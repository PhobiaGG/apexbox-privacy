ApexBox Companion Account Deletion

If you would like to request the deletion of your ApexBox Companion account and all associated data, please send an email to:

apexbox@apexboxofficial.com

Please use the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address you used to sign up for your account.

What data will be deleted?

Upon processing your request, we will permanently delete all of your personal data from our servers, including:

  • Your account credentials (email and authenticated user ID)
  • Your user profile (display name, profile picture)
  • Your custom crew data
  • All of your GPS track replays and performance session data
  • Your leaderboard entries

Please note that we may retain anonymized, non-personal data for statistical and analytical purposes.

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
